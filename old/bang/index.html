BANG! The dice game

Dice

  • 1 - Close shot: Choose the player next to you on your left or right (1 position away). That player loses one life point.
  • 2 - Long shot: Choose the player 2 positions away on your left or right. That player loses one life point.
  • 3 - Arrow: You must resolve this die immediately when rolled. Take one arrow token (one per arrow rolled) and keep it in front of you. You may roll this die again if you have re-rolls left. If you take the last arrow, each player loses one life point for each arrow they have. Then all players discard their arrows. Resume your turn.
  • 4 - Dynamite: This die cannot be re-rolled. If you roll three or more Dynamites, your turn ends immediately and you lose one life point. But, all your other dice results are still resolved as normal.
  • 5 - Beer: Choose ANY player. That player gains one life point. A player can never gain more life points than the number they had at the beginning of the game.
  • 6 - Gatling: If you roll three or more Gatlings you activate the Gatling Gun. Each of the other players loses one life point. Also, you discard all of your arrows.

Character Cards

  • A♠ - BART CASSIDY (8): You may take an arrow instead of losing a life point (except to Indians or Dynamite).
  • 2♠ - BLACK JACK (8): You may re-roll dynamites (unless if you roll three or more!).
  • 3♠ - CALAMITY JANET (8): You can use "close shots" as "long shots" and vice-versa
  • 4♠ - EL GRINGO (7): When a player makes you lose one or more life points, he must take an arrow.
  • 5♠ - JESSE JONES (9): If you have four life points or less, you gain two if you use "beer" for yourself
  • 6♠ - JOURDONNAIS (7): You never lose more than one life point to Indians.
  • 7♠ - KIT CARLSON (7): For each "gatling" you may discard one arrow from any player. You may choose to discard your own arrows. If you roll three "gatling", you discard all your own arrows, plus three from any player(s) (of course, you still deal one damage to each other player).
  • 8♠ - LUCKY DUKE (8): You may make one extra re-roll.
  • A♥ - PAUL REGRET (9): You never lose life points to the Gatling Gun.
  • 2♥ - PEDRO RAMIREZ (8): Each time you lose a life point, you may discard one of your arrows.
  • 3♥ - ROSE DOOLAN (9): You may use "close shot" or "long shot" for players sitting one place further.
  • 4♥ - SID KETCHUM (8): At the beginning of your turn, any player of your choice gains one life point.
  • 5♥ - SLAB THE KILLER (8): Once per turn, you can use a "beer" to double a "close shot" or "long shot". If you do, it does not provide any life points.
  • 6♥ - SUZY LAFAYETTE (8): If you didn’t roll any "close shot" or "long shot" at the end of your turn, you gain two life points.
  • 7♥ - VULTURE SAM (9): Investigate each player who is holding a gun.
  • 8♥ - WILLY THE KID (8): You only need to "gatling" use the Gatling Gun. (It can only be activated once).
