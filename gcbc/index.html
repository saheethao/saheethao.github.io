Equipment Cards

  • A♠ - Wiretap: Investigate any two players
  • 2♠ - Flashbang: Shuffle all your integrity cards, look at them, and place them face-down on the table in any order
  • 3♠ - Report Audit: Each player who has no face-up integrity cards must choose an integrity card to turn face up.
  • 4♠ - Polygraph: Choose a player a view all their integrity cards then show them all of your integrity cards.
  • 5♠ - Evidence bag: Choose a player and take that player's equipment card.
  • 6♠ - Truth Serum: Choose a player. That player chooses one face-down integrity card to turn face-up.
  • 7♠ - Metal detector: investigate each player who is holding a gun.
  • 8♠ - Blackmail: Choose two integrity cards held by other players. Those players exchange integrity cards.
  • A♥ - Blackmail: Choose two integrity cards held by other players. Those players exchange integrity cards.
  • 2♥ - Planted Evidence: Choose a player. Crooked = honest. Honest = crooked.
  • 3♥ - Coffee: After the current turn ends, play moves immediately to your turn.
  • 4♥ - Surveillance Camera: Use immediately after a player is investigated. That player must turn the integrity card that was viewed face-up on the table.
  • 5♥ - Defibrillator: Revive a player who was eliminated. Choose and discard two of their integrity cards. Their role is determined by one integrity card for the rest of the game.
  • 6♥ - Taser: Steal a gun from any player. Only use on your turn. This counts as your action.
  • 7♥ - Restraining Order: Use when a player shoots their gun. They must chose a diffrent player and immediatly aim at and shoot that player instead.
  • 8♥ - K9 Unit: Choose a player. If they are holding a gun, they drop it. If it is currently that player's turn they cannot equip a gun this turn.
